Chao Mung Den Voi Cong Doan Thanh Nien

Chung ta duoc goi de yeu thuong, phuc vu va chia se Phuc Am cua Chua Giasu cho ca the gioi.

Ve Chung Toi

Cong Doan Thanh Nien Tin Lanh la mot nhom cac thanh nien tran day nang luong, duc tin va tinh yeu thuong.

Cac Gia Tri Cot Loi:

Duc Tin

Su Menh

Hoat Dong Cua Chung Toi

1. Tho Phuong va Cau Nguyen

Chung toi tap trung tho phuong Chua Giasu voi tam hon chan thanh, hat nhung bai thanh ca va cau nguyen cung nhau vao moi Chu Nhat.

2. Hoc Tap Kinh Thanh

Cac buoi hoc tap Kinh Thanh duoc to chuc de giup thanh nien hieu sau hon ve Duc Chua va loi day cua Ngan.

3. Hoat Dong Tu Thien

Chung toi thuong xuyen tham gia cac hoat dong tu thien nhu phat qua cho nguoi ngheo, giup do cac gia dinh gap kho khan.

4. Cac Buoi Gap Go Xa Hoi

Ben canh nhung hoat dong ton giao, chung toi cung to chuc cac buoi gap go xa hoi de gan ket tinh ban.
